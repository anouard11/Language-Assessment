Core Concepts

1 / 7

1. Testing

01

Definition (Brown, 2004)

"A test is a method of measuring a person's ability, knowledge, or performance in a given domain."

Attributes

  • • Formal & Structured
  • • Time-limited
  • • Produces scores

Purpose

  • • Measure learners’ ability
  • • Check learning results

Relationship

  • • Produces measurement
  • • Part of assessment

2. Assessment

02

Definition (Brown, 2004)

"Assessment is an ongoing process that encompasses a much wider domain."

Attributes

  • • Continuous
  • • Formal & Informal

Purpose

  • • Monitor progress
  • • Improve teaching

Relationship

  • • Includes testing
  • • Helps teaching

3. Evaluation

03

Definition (Brown & Abeywickrama, 2010)

"Evaluation involves the interpretation of measurement results and making judgments about students' performance."

Attributes

  • • Judgment-based
  • • Decision-making

Purpose

  • • Judge performance

Relationship

  • • Follows assessment

4. Quiz

A short classroom test used for formative assessment.

5. Measurement

The quantification of learners' performance (scores).

6. Teaching

Guiding and facilitating learning.

7. Examination

Formal measurement held at the end of a term.

References

  • Bachman, L. F. (1990). Fundamental considerations in language testing. Oxford University Press.
  • Brown, H. D. (2004). Language assessment: Principles and classroom practices. Pearson Education.
  • Brown, H. D. (2007). Principles of language learning and teaching. Pearson Education.
